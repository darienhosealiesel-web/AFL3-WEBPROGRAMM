PHOTOGRAPHY

MY ARTISTIC SIDE

Having joined a photography class in senior high, I have since developed an interest in photography. Whether using a phone or a camera, I enjoy capturing scenery and abstract moments. It's a way for me to express myself and see the world from different perspectives. I think photography is a great way to capture memories and emotions in a unique way. Because when words cant express how you feel, a photo can do the job.

TRAVELLING

MY ADVENTUROUS SIDE

I now enjoy the freedom of traveling. Exploring new places with friends and family is what makes me happiest. Exploring new places and experiencing different cultures is something I really enjoy. Traveling allows me to step out of my comfort zone, meet new people, and create unforgettable memories. Whether it's a beach getaway or a mountain adventure, I love the thrill of discovering new destinations and immersing myself in different cultures.

COFFEE ENTHUSIAST

MY OBSESSION

I learned to appreciate coffee over time. Its bitterness became something I enjoy, especially after long nights. Coffee has become a part of my daily routine, providing comfort and a moment of relaxation. I love trying different coffee blends and brewing methods, and it's always a great way to connect with friends over a cup of coffee. Whether it's a simple black coffee or a fancy latte, I find joy in savoring the rich flavors and aromas that coffee has to offer.
